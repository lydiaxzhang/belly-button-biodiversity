Welcome to the Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard, hosted by Prominent Microbiology Laboratories. Our mission is to discover and document the numerous baterial species that are unknown to humankind and further study those that are not well known. In partnership with Improbable Beef, a start-up which researches meat-substitues, Prominenet Microbiology Laboratories has set out to discover a new bacteria that synthesizes proteins which taste like beef. This dashboard showcases the data collected from study participants' navels - specifically their belly buttons. Please use the below interface to select the partipant by their "Test Subject ID No." and view their demographic information, the top 10 bacteria cultures found, their frequency of belly button washing, and a visualization of the amount of bacteria cultures found per sample.
